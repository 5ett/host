Bored at home? got some free time?

Anime101 allows you to submit anime recommendations and playlists for new and old weebs alike. By creating a community of co-dependence, we hope to strengthen the bonds which connect us as weebs and believers in anime. Your playlists will be rated by those who use them so others might know or find you easily. You can garner fans but ultimately serve the one community: The Anime community

Got a legit and unbiased take on anime?

You can also submit fun anime review articles which will be featured on our blog page for all weebs to see. Take it as the Hokage Stone Heads in Konoha, everyone will see it as long as they enter this space! We'll also make sure hate speech and sensitive content will be treated as necessary. Besides that, almost all content will be allowed for fans of every genre and type of anime to enjoy interacting with articles and playlists. Yes, after you use or subscribe to a playlist, you could submit your experience for the XP section of the blog page.

Heard any "fact" you're not sure of?

From Twitter to Tiktok, weeb creators have muddled their content with "facts" that cannot be proven with evidence. Huh? you got that right. People will say anything for a following these days and honestly, we could do better as a community. It's why we came up with the Fact Board section. We'll provide verifiable facts from any and all anime with links to interviews, trustworthy articles and Youtube videos. You can submit interesting anime facts you find with links. If they're verified, they'll be featured, you'll be credited and links to your socials will be added so fellow weebs can connect with you. Increase your credibility in the anime community and become a reviewer for Anime101. The moment you provide new and verifiable facts to the fact board, a 5 star meter will start running and with more facts delivered, the higher your ratings and credibility go! Fun right?

If you've got any of the above, check the submit page!

From facts to reviews to playlists, you're free to send as many as you'd like. Really, there's no limit to what you can submit as far as they're clean, helpful and interactively fun. Go to the submit page and follow the guidelines to get your articles and fact findings featured on Anime101
